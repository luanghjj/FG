3
much / many / a little / a few
10 P.
3.1 – Fill in 6 P.
  1. We don't have much sugar left. (sugar = không đếm được, câu phủ định)
  2. How many guests are coming? (guests = đếm được số nhiều)
  3. I need a few minutes (three or four). (minutes = đếm được, số ít)
  4. There were many customers. (full → số đông, đếm được)
  5. There is only a little milk. (milk = không đếm được, số ít)
  6. He doesn't earn much money. (money = không đếm được, phủ định)
3.2 – a few / few · a little / little 4 P.

a few / a little = some, enough (positive). few / little (without "a") = almost none (negative).

Example: She has a few friends (some) ↔ She has few friends (almost none). / a little time (some) ↔ little time (hardly any).

⚠️ "a" makes it positive; without "a" it sounds negative.
4
Present & future tenses
11 P.
4.1 – Correct tense 6 P.
  1. Our shop opens at 8 a.m. every morning. (every morning → simple present, ngôi 3 +s)
  2. I am serving a customer. (now → present progressive)
  3. We are going to lose money this year. (bằng chứng "dropping every month" → going to)
  4. The phone is ringing. (Listen! ngay lúc này → present progressive)
  5. She speaks three languages. (sự thật / khả năng → simple present)
  6. We are going to have a busy day. (bằng chứng "look at the queue" → going to)
4.2 – going-to future 5 P.

Use the going-to future for planned actions (an intention you already have) and for a logical consequence you can already see.

Form: am/is/are + going to + infinitive. Example: We are going to hire two new waiters next month.
