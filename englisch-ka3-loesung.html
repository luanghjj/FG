1
Telephoning
15 P.
1.1 – Sample dialogue 8 P.

R: Good morning, Green Hotel. Anna speaking. How can I help you?
C: This is Mr Sánchez. I'd like to speak to Ms Bauer, please.
R: May I ask what it is about?
C: It's about a booking.
R: I'm afraid Ms Bauer is not available right now. Would you like to leave a message?
C: Yes, please ask her to call me back.
R: Of course. I'll pass on your message. Thank you for calling. Goodbye.

(Điểm: chào + tên công ty → hỏi người gọi → báo vắng mặt → nhận lời nhắn → chào kết thúc lịch sự)

1.3 – Three call types + example 3 P.
  • Information call — e.g. asking for opening hours / confirming an appointment.
  • Problem call — e.g. the delivery is late.
  • Complaint call — e.g. an angry client about a broken product.
2
Dealing with complaints
14 P.
2.1 – Customer (C) or Waiter (W)? 5 P.
  • "I'm terribly sorry about that." → W
  • "Excuse me, this isn't what I ordered." → C
  • "I'll bring you a new one right away." → W
  • "I'd like to speak to the manager, please." → C
  • "Thank you for your patience." → W
2.2 – Waiter response (long wait) 3 P.

Sample: "I'm very sorry for the long wait. I understand your frustration and I'll check with the kitchen immediately."

(Xin lỗi + thể hiện thấu hiểu + kiểm tra bếp ngay)

2.3 – Four-step formula + stay calm 6 P.

Formula: Listen → Apologize → Solve → Thank / follow-up.

Why stay calm: Staying calm keeps the situation under control and shows the guest that the problem is taken seriously, so the guest stays satisfied and the restaurant keeps a good image.

(Bình tĩnh giúp kiểm soát tình huống, khách thấy được tôn trọng, giữ hình ảnh nhà hàng.)

4
Present & future tenses
11 P.
4.1 – Correct tense 6 P.
  1. The meeting starts at 9 o'clock tomorrow. (lịch cố định → simple present)
  2. Right now the manager is interviewing a candidate. (right now → present progressive)
  3. It is going to open a new branch soon. (kế hoạch → going to)
  4. I always check my e-mails in the morning. (always → simple present)
  5. Be careful — you are going to drop those glasses! (hệ quả nhìn thấy trước → going to)
  6. We do not work (don't work) on Sundays. (thói quen phủ định → simple present với don't)
4.2 – Two own sentences 5 P.

Sample: Simple present (routine): "I work in a restaurant every weekend."
Present progressive (now): "At the moment I am learning English for my job."

(1 câu thói quen ở hiện tại đơn + 1 câu đang xảy ra ở hiện tại tiếp diễn)
