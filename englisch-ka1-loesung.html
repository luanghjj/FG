Telephoning
15 P.
1.1 – Match situation ↔ phrase 5 P.
  • 1 → b (Good morning, Smart Logistics. Anna speaking.)
  • 2 → c (I'll put you through to Mr Brown.)
  • 3 → d (Could you hold on, please?)
  • 4 → e (I'm afraid he is not in the office right now.)
  • 5 → a (Would you like to leave a message?)
1.2 – Rude → polite 4 P.
RudePolite (sample)
Give me your number.Could you please give me your number?
What do you want?How can I help you?
He's not here.I'm afraid he is not available right now.
I can't hear you!Could you please repeat that?

(Câu lịch sự thường dùng: Could you please … ? / I'm afraid …)

1.3 – Dialogue order 3 P.
  1. Good morning, Tech Solutions. How can I help you?
  2. This is Anna Schmidt speaking. I'd like to speak to Mr Miller, please.
  3. Certainly. May I ask who is calling?
  4. I'll put you through.

(Trình tự: chào + đề nghị giúp → người gọi nêu yêu cầu → hỏi tên → chuyển máy)

1.4 – Take a message (2 sentences) 3 P.

Sample: "I'm afraid Mr Brown is not available right now. Would you like to leave a message?" — "I'll make sure he calls you back about the urgent delivery as soon as possible."

(Điểm: xin lỗi/thông báo vắng mặt + đề nghị nhắn tin/ hứa gọi lại về việc giao hàng gấp)

Dealing with complaints
14 P.
2.1 – Professional responses 4 P.
ComplaintSample response
The soup is cold.I'm terribly sorry. I'll take it back and bring you a fresh one right away.
Waiting over 30 minutes.I understand your frustration. I'll check with the kitchen immediately.
This isn't what I ordered.I apologize for the mistake. I'll bring you the correct dish at once.
Allergic to nuts, nuts in dish.I'm terribly sorry. I'll replace the dish immediately and inform the chef.

(Luôn: xin lỗi → xử lý ngay. Với Allergy = an toàn trên hết, đổi món + báo bếp.)

2.2 – Wrong bill dialogue 4 P.

Waiter (1): I'm sorry. Let me check it for you.

Waiter (2): You're right — I'll correct the bill immediately.

(Kiểm tra bình tĩnh + sửa hóa đơn ngay, không tranh cãi)

2.3 – Four steps + why not argue 6 P.

Formula: Listen → Apologize → Solve (replace / fix / move) → Thank / follow-up.

Why not argue: Arguing makes the guest angrier and damages the image of the restaurant; staying calm and offering a solution keeps the customer satisfied.

(Không tranh cãi vì làm khách giận hơn và hại hình ảnh nhà hàng.)

3
much / many / a little / a few
10 P.
3.1 – Fill in 6 P.
  1. There isn't much water left. (water = không đếm được)
  2. She has a few close friends (only two or three). (a few = một vài; "only two or three" → số ít)
  3. There are many people waiting outside (crowded). (many = nhiều; "crowded" → số đông)
  4. I only have a little time. (time = không đếm được)
  5. How much money do you need? (money = không đếm được)
  6. Could I have a little advice? (advice = không đếm được)
3.2 – Rule much vs many 4 P.

much + uncountable nouns (water, money, time). Example: much water.

many + countable plural nouns (people, books). Example: many books.

⚠️ countable → many / a few · uncountable → much / a little.
4
Present & future tenses
11 P.
4.1 – Correct tense 6 P.
  1. I go to work every day. (every day → simple present)
  2. I am talking to a customer at the moment. (at the moment → present progressive)
  3. Next year the company is going to hire five new employees. (next year, kế hoạch → going to)
  4. The train leaves at 11. (lịch cố định → simple present)
  5. Look at those clouds — it is going to rain. (hệ quả nhìn thấy trước → going to)
  6. She usually answers the phone in English. (usually → simple present, ngôi 3 +s)
4.2 – Signal words + timetable 5 P.
  • Simple present: every day / always / often / usually (one is enough).
  • Present progressive: now / at the moment / currently / right now.
  • Fixed timetable: uses the simple present (e.g. The train leaves at 11).
